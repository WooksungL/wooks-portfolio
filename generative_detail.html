Architecture as Process: Designing Across Tools and Logics

This project was not about producing a single final outcome, but rather about experimenting with and expanding architectural thinking through a process-oriented exploration across diverse design tools and logics. Tools such as ComfyUI, Grasshopper, Houdini, Python, and Unreal Engine were not merely instruments‚Äîthey each demanded their own unique way of thinking and operational logic. Engaging directly with those differences became a way of learning new architectural languages. From AI-generated floor plans to parametric volume construction, real-time feedback systems, and volumetric refinement, the process was not about converging on one perfect solution but about opening up multiple experimental pathways. Design here was not a fixed result, but an evolving structure emerging from the interaction between tools. By navigating and negotiating the tension between heterogeneous systems, this project became a direct exploration into new design methodologies and expanded architectural thought.

Should we design architecture as a finished product, or as an evolving system in progress?
