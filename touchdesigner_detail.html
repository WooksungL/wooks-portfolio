2. Cellular Automata

This project visualizes the life and decay of particles in a self-organizing system using a custom GLSL shader in TouchDesigner. The vertex shader reads cell data (alive, life, state) from a texture buffer, then positions and colors each particle in 3D space based on its state. The fragment shader performs real-time lighting calculations (Phong model) and integrates fog, alpha, and camera-based lighting. All visual behavior is fully GPU-accelerated and procedurally defined‚Äîthere is no simulation in SOPs or TOPs. The result is a minimal, but computationally complete ecosystem rendered entirely on the graphics pipeline.
